Let $Y = Pois(\lambda)$

The pdf is:
$f(y) = e^{-\lambda}\lambda^y/y!$ 
- for y = 0,1,2,...

Change $f(y)$ into an exponential form
$f(y) = exp[ylog(\lambda) -\lambda-log(y!)]$
This is now a form the [[Exponential Family]] requires

$\theta = log(\lambda)$
$b(\theta)= e^\theta$
$a(\phi) = \phi = 1$
$c(y,\phi) = -log(y!)$
